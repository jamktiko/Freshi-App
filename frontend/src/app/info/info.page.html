What is Freshi?

Ever found a forgotten bag of rice at the back of your cupboard? We’ve all been there. Freshi is a simple tool that helps you track best-before dates in one easy place.

Just log your items, and we’ll send you a friendly nudge before they expire. It’s the easiest way to save money and reduce food waste — one pantry at a time!
